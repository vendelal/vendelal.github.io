Dinner

01

Ingredients

  • 2-3lbs potatoes (fingerling, baby or waxy chopped into 1 in pieces) 
  • 1 big head of kale 
  • 5 carrots 
  • 6 celery
  •  3 medium onions 
  • 2 leeks 
  • 1 garlic 
  • 1 pint heavy cream 
  • 1 gallons chicken stock
  •  2 lbs 85/15 ground beef 
  • 1.5 cup grated salty strong cheese (parmesan, romano, asiago) 
  • Oil 
  • Salt
  •  Pepper

Tasty Soup

Joe Maki

Directions

MEATBALLS

Break up hamburger in a mixing bowel 

Sprinkle 1 teaspoon salt over the meat 

Sprinkle grated cheese over the meat 

Mix well 

Form into large grape sized meatballs

SOUP BASE

Chop celery, carrots, onions, leeks, and garlic, salt pepper Saute in oil until the veggies start getting soft and aromatic Add chicken stock, potatoes, kale

Simmer for a while until potatoes are cooked Reduce heat to low Add heavy cream, and salt to taste

Let it sit for an hour

Breakfast

02

Ingredients

  • Portuguese rolls
  • 1 ½ - 2 eggs per sandwich
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Joe's Better-Than-Clover's Breakfast Sandwich

Joe Maki

Directions

1. Butter and grill rolls on inside in a pan

2. Bring pot of water to boil with eggs in there, take off right away once you have a roaring boil and leave for 4-5 min, then cool down immediately with cold water/ice

3. Assemble sandwiches, cheese on bottom, then tomatoes salted, then eggs salted

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary

  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Red wine
  • Good balsamic
  • Rosemary
  • Beef, duck or veal Demi glace
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper

Grilled Duck Moullard in Red Wine Sauce

Lindsey Gusenburg

Directions

Duck

Score the duck fat/skin side

Add a good amount of salt and pepper and rub it into the fat/skin side (and the meat side)

Add pieces of rosemary between the cut fat

Grill duck skin side down first, managing flare-ups, until skin is crisp (about 5 minutes)

Flip duck and cook until rare to medium rare

Sauce

Sauté the shallots in a medium saucepan with the oil over a high heat for about 3 mins until lightly browned, stirring often. Season with ground black pepper and add the garlic and rosemary. Continue cooking for a further 3 mins, stirring often to prevent the shallots burning.

Pour in the vinegar and cook until evaporated away to a syrup, then pour in the wine and cook until reduced by two thirds.

Dinner

04

Ingredients

  • Rub
  • 1lb monkfish cut into half inch thick nuggets
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp old bay
  • 1/8 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

  • Flour
  • 3 tbsp all purpose
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Monkfish Nuggets

Joe Maki

Directions

Toss nuggets in the rub, massage in gently

Let sit 5 minutes

Toss in bag with flour

Take out of bag and shake off excess flour, let sit 10 minutes

Brown nuggets in oil and butter

Take out

In 1/4 inch oil to pan, heat to 350

Fry both sides for 1 minute
