About Karina Larsson

Bringing out beauty and comfort in your home just like you’ve always envisioned… that’s my mission at Livingetc., my interior design firm which offers a full range of design services for residential homes.

Collaboration is integral to my process. I listen to your objectives and your design preferences, and then use my adeptness with color, use of lighting and my eye for proportion and balance to bring out the best in your home.

During my many years as an interior designer, I’ve developed an eclectic style. I design to your aesthetic, be it traditional, classic or contemporary. And, with an eye for composition and scale, I can integrate touches of each to add a focal point or an element of interest to the décor of your home.

I understand color and recognize its importance as a component of interior design. Through color, I create an environment that brings out good energy and sets your mood— foremost, one that’s a reflection of you.

My designs are anchored with clean lines. A clean, spatially organized look captures and projects the essence of your home and lends itself to the comfortable and functional feel of your space. This same purge/declutter approach also impacts staging, another Livingetc. service that benefits clients who are selling their homes.

Research has proven that staging increases your home’s selling price and significantly shortens its sales cycle. Clients who have used my services for staging—particularly those who initially have had problems marketing their homes—say staging has upped their home’s buyer appeal, and resulted in improved ROI.
